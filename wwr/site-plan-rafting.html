Purpose

Whitewater Rafting is a growing business that is all about the adventure experience. Our customers are out there looking for thrilling experiences and it is time for us to go directly to them through theire devices with our own site. We look to increase our renown in order to increse our clientele and build more revenues.

Audience

Who are the target customers? Our target audience are the young adults that are now part of the labor force, around 25 to 30 years old that in this moment may not have all the responsabilities our dues of supporting a family. What do they want? They are looking for fresh great advetures. How will my audience usually access my site (desktop, laptop, mobile device)? Most of them are going to access the site through a mobile device. What do they expect to find or what questions will be answered by them accessing the website? They need a site for them to get to know that a rafting adventure is exactly what they need to get out of the routine and to be refresh.
